Oluseyi Boroffice, MBA, MPA, BSEE

Oluseyi Boroffice is a visionary leader and dynamic strategist with a distinguished career spanning energy infrastructure, real estate technology, and global development. Architect Olubunmi Abiola Ayeni

Architect Olubunmi Abiola Ayeni is a seasoned professional with over three decades of dedicated experience in architecture and the building industry in Nigeria. A graduate of the University of Ife—now Obafemi Awolowo University—he holds both BSc and MSc degrees in Architecture.
