The Founders

Oluseyi Boroffice, MBA, MPA, BSEE

Oluseyi Boroffice is a visionary leader and dynamic strategist with a distinguished career spanning energy infrastructure, real estate technology, and global development. With advanced degrees from MIT and Harvard, and a foundation in electrical engineering from Northeastern University, he combines deep technical expertise with business acumen to drive innovation and growth across diverse sectors. As Founder and CEO of Gidillo Property Hub, Oluseyi is transforming Africa’s real estate landscape through technology, streamlining transactions and boosting transparency. His impact in the energy sector is equally profound—he played a key role in Nigeria’s historic utility privatization, led development of multi-million-dollar renewable energy projects in conflict-affected regions, and crafted power generation strategies that attracted significant investment. Oluseyi’s global footprint includes consulting in India to empower marginalized communities, pioneering satellite communications strategy for Africa in Bahrain, and engineering breakthrough testing algorithms at Qualcomm that saved millions of dollars for the company. A skilled negotiator and policy analyst, he has successfully raised hundreds of millions in infrastructure funding, managed high-stakes government relations, and developed market strategies that span continents. His career is defined by purpose-driven innovation, cross-cultural leadership, and a relentless drive to solve complex challenges at the intersection of technology, policy, and impact. Beyond his professional achievements, Oluseyi is a published author and committed missionary, bringing God centered leadership to every endeavor.

Architect Olubunmi Abiola Ayeni

Architect Olubunmi Abiola Ayeni is a seasoned professional with over three decades of dedicated experience in architecture and the building industry in Nigeria. A graduate of the University of Ife—now Obafemi Awolowo University—he holds both BSc and MSc degrees in Architecture. He is a proud Fellow of the prestigious Nigerian Institute of Architects (FNIA), a recognition that honors his outstanding contributions to the profession and his unwavering commitment to architectural excellence. As Managing Director of The Builtforms Nigeria Limited since 2000, Ayeni has led the delivery of transformative projects across Nigeria—including luxury residential developments in Lagos and Abuja, institutional buildings for Caleb University, and commercial architecture for major banks. His professional expertise spans master planning, project management, and innovative design solutions, supported by proficiency in leading software such as AutoCAD, MS Project, Excel, Outlook, and 3D Studio. Beyond design and construction, Ayeni is devoted to mentorship and knowledge-sharing. He lectured at the Federal Polytechnic, Ede, and continues to support aspiring architects through internships and industry engagement. His legacy of leadership extends to active roles in professional bodies, alumni networks, and community development initiatives—from infrastructure projects in Ekiti State to youth empowerment through NGOs. His career is a blueprint of vision, integrity, and impact—framing not just buildings, but the future of architecture in Nigeria.
