About CMN

For Builders, By Builders.

Construction Materials Network or CMN is a company dedicated to helping builders build better, stronger and safe building or structures, provide information, articles, new technology trends that currently exist in the construction industry

Our Mission

To provide resources for builders so as to make planning, procuring and building easier, faster, and at a reduced cost by using technology. To act as a market nexus for builders, property developers, government, buyers and real-estate investors in Nigeria and Africa.

Our Vision

The Vision of the Company is to make housing safer, more plentiful and affordable for Nigerians and Africans by leveraging technology in a sustainable way.
